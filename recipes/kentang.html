Description

This is crispy fries

Ingridients

  • Potato
  • OIl
  • Sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. First Cut potato into a long smaller size
  2. Heat up the pan and put the oil
  3. Put the potato into the pan
  4. Put the cooked potato into a plate
  5. Put sauce and salt to the potato to give some flavour
  6. The crispy fries is ready to serve
