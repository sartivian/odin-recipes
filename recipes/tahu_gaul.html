Tahu Gaul

Description

This is recipe for the best tahu gaul in the world

Ingridients

  • Tahu
  • Oil
  • MSG
  • Flour
  • Chili powder

Steps

  1. First put the flour into bowl
  2. Slice the tahu into a small cube shape
  3. Put the tahu into the bowl
  4. After that put the tahu into heated pan with oil
  5. Put the cooked tahu into a plate
  6. Put MSG and chili powder
  7. The best tahu gaul in the world is ready to eat
