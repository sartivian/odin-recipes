Basreng

Description

This is recipe for make a delicious spicy basreng

Ingridients

  • Meatball
  • Oil
  • Chili Powder
  • MSG

Steps

  1. Cut the meatball to be long small size
  2. Heat up the pan and add oil
  3. Put the cutted meatball into the pan
  4. After cooked take it to a plate
  5. Put in chili powder and MSG to the plate
  6. Enjoy the Scpicy Delicious Basreng
